Procedures Submitted to Medicare Part B by Advanced Practice Providers between 2010 and 2024

Our software calculates the number and proportion of procedures submitted to Medicare Part B by advance practice providers between 2010 and 2024. Advanced practice providers include physician assistants, nurse practitioners, certified registered nurse anesthetists, certified clinical nurse specialists, and certified nurse midwives. The denominator of the proportion includes claims submitted by advanced practice providers and physicians.

Enter up to twenty-five procedure codes, each separated by a semicolon with no spaces (e.g., 31237;S2342;...). If more than one procedure code is entered, the output calculates the number and proportion of procedures submitted across all entered procedures codes, not each individual code.
